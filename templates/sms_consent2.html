SMS Consent Policy

Chesera LLC sends SMS messages only after obtaining customer consent. Customers initiate the conversation first and SMS communication is limited to customer support, appointment updates and service-related responses.

  • Customers initiate the SMS conversation.
  • Messages are sent only for customer support.
  • No unsolicited marketing or promotional messages are sent.
  • Reply STOP to opt out.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.

Verbal Consent Process

Before sending any SMS messages, our support representative asks for the customer's permission.

Support Representative:

"May we send you SMS messages regarding your current support request? These messages may include support updates, appointment updates and responses to your inquiries. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance. Do you agree to receive these messages?"

Customer:

"Yes."

SMS messages are only sent after the customer provides verbal consent. Support Representative records the customer's verbal consent before SMS communication begins. Consent records are retained for compliance purposes.

Customer-Initiated Conversations

Customers always initiate the conversation by contacting our business first by:

  • Sending an SMS to our support number
  • Calling our support team
  • Submitting a support request
  • Requesting customer assistance
